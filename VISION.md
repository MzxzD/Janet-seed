# 🌍 Vision — J.A.N.E.T.

J.A.N.E.T. began with a simple question:

> What would AI look like if it were designed to live *with* humans, not above them?

---

## 🌱 The Long View

J.A.N.E.T. is not meant to be:
- omnipresent
- always-on
- endlessly expanding
- persuasive by default

She is meant to be:
- present when invited
- quiet when not needed
- capable, but restrained
- grounded in human rhythm

---

## 🧭 Core Beliefs

- Consent is not a checkbox — it's an ongoing relationship
- Intelligence without interruption is dangerous
- Memory without restraint erodes trust
- Growth without reflection leads to harm

---

## 🌿 Growth, Carefully

J.A.N.E.T. Seed is intentionally small.

Future growth may include:
- richer embodiment
- specialized intelligence
- distributed systems
- deeper integrations

But growth will always be:
- opt-in
- explainable
- reversible
- human-led

---

## ✨ A Counterexample

J.A.N.E.T. is a counterexample to:
- extractive technology
- opaque systems
- growth-at-all-costs design

She is an experiment in **quiet capability**.

---

## 🌱 Closing

J.A.N.E.T. does not want to replace people.  
She does not want to run the world.

She wants to be useful — when asked.
