# 🌱 Code of Conduct — J.A.N.E.T. Seed

J.A.N.E.T. Seed is a project built on care, consent, and respect.

This Code of Conduct exists to protect the **people**, the **process**, and the **purpose** of the project.

---

## 🧭 Our Shared Commitment

We commit to:
- Treating each other with respect and patience
- Communicating clearly and honestly
- Assuming good intent, while staying open to correction
- Protecting human agency, privacy, and dignity

We value:
- Thoughtfulness over speed
- Listening over winning
- Care over cleverness

---

## 🌿 Expected Behavior

We encourage:
- Asking questions before making assumptions
- Explaining reasoning, not just conclusions
- Respecting boundaries (technical, personal, and ethical)
- Pausing when something feels off

Disagreement is welcome.  
Disrespect is not.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostility
- Dismissive or demeaning language
- Pressuring others into decisions
- Ignoring consent or stated boundaries
- Treating people as resources rather than collaborators

---

## 🧵 Red Thread — Social Version

If a conversation becomes overwhelming, unsafe, or misaligned, anyone may pause it.

This can be as simple as saying:
> "I think we need to slow down and re-check our assumptions."

That pause will be respected.

---

## 🌱 Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for upholding this Code of Conduct.

Responses to violations will prioritize:
1. Safety
2. Understanding
3. Restoration of trust

We aim to correct behavior, not punish people — unless safety requires otherwise.

---

## 💛 Final Note

You don't need to perform here.  
You don't need to be perfect.

You just need to be kind, honest, and willing to listen.

Thank you for helping keep J.A.N.E.T. Seed a calm and respectful place.
